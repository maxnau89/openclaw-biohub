# Disclaimer

**This software is not medical advice and is not a medical device.**

`openclaw-biohub` is a personal data-handling tool for self-tracking
hobbyists. It is provided as-is for **informational and educational
purposes only**.

## What this means concretely

- The dashboard, the ML pattern engine, the agent persona, and every
  recommendation surfaced by them are derived from your own historical
  data. They do not constitute clinical guidance.
- The reference ranges shipped in fixtures and parsing helpers are
  generic adult ranges. Your personal range may differ. Lab-issued
  reference ranges always take precedence over anything in this code.
- "Anomaly", "flagged", and similar labels in the UI mean "statistically
  unusual versus your own baseline" — **not** "medically concerning".
- The authors and contributors are **not** physicians, nurses,
  registered dieticians, or licensed clinicians, and this code carries
  no implied clinical accuracy.

## Do not use this to

- Diagnose any condition.
- Treat, cure, mitigate, or prevent any disease.
- Make decisions about prescription medication.
- Replace conversations with your doctor.
- Provide health advice to anyone else as if it were authoritative.

## If something looks off

If `openclaw-biohub` flags something — or if you feel unwell regardless
of what the data says — **talk to a real clinician**. The data here is
useful context for that conversation, not a substitute for it.
